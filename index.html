About

Hi, my name is Heather and I'm a third year Computer Science student at the University of California, Santa Barbara. I am highly motivated and determined, and my biggest passion is discovering new hobbies that I enjoy and obsessively working to improve my skills.

In Winter quarter of 2022, I interned as a software engineer intern at Wish, specifically on the Merchant Web Infrastructure team. I contributed greatly to the migration of the entire frontend codebase from a monolithic structure to a NextJS microservice by writing codemod scripts in Typescript to ensure successful migration of dependencies and NextJS-specific features.

In Summer of 2021, I worked at ServiceNow as a software engineer intern on the Backend Applications team. Along with creating a logging utility function for the backend MID servers, I learned a lot about the Agile Software Development Lifecycle from creating a design document to demoing finished functionality during sprint meetings.

On campus, I was previously a part of the Avionics Team of the Rocket Propulsion Laboratory at UCSB. As a software developer, I led a project creating a desktop application that displays incoming telemetry data (such as orientation, angular velocity, altitude, and current state) during the flight of a Thrust Vector Controlled rocket, implemented using ReactJS and Electron. And in the past, I worked as an Undergraduate Researcher at the UCSB Washburn Lab where I built a mobile-friendly web application to display vector overlays of ocean currents and manipulate map features for field researchers across the nation.

Aside from academic and professional experiences, I am very involved within the engineering community. As an active member of Theta Tau, a professional engineering fraternity, I was head of the rush committee that organized events for Fall 2021 rush. I'm also a member of the Society of Women Engineers, where I previously held a board position as the Treasurer.

As mentioned before, I love picking up new hobbies! I enjoy longboarding, playing sports (tennis, basketball, volleyball), and playing guitar. For Summer 2022, I'm excited to be working at Datadog as a software engineer intern in New York!
